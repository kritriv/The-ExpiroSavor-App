ExpiroSavor App
Save and Donate

The problem it solves :
1. Food is one of the most basic necessities that an individual requires to survive. However, with poverty persisting, many underprivileged individuals even find it hard to receive a single meal in the day.
2. India is one of the countries that faces the challenge of food insecurity and malnutrition.
3. When the medicine gets expired. It is very difficult to recycle it and very harmful to the environment.
4. Poor People did not receive the one-time meal.
  
Challenges we ran into when we were using the Python goopy library it's difficult to implement while shorting product items according to the nearest location of the user.

Technologies we used : Html, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery, Bootstrap, Python, Flask, SQLlite, API
